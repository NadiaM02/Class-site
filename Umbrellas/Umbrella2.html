SEAHORSE FACTS

Habitat

on the volcanic islands of indonesia komodo dragons live in the lower forests, grassland, savanna, scrubland and tropical forests. They prefer the hot and dry lands of these places.

Population

There are about 3,000 to 5,000 komodo dragons left in the world. This qualifies them as an endangered species.

Offspring

Komodo dragons mating season happens between the months of May and august. Komodo Dragons lay around 20 eggs and are incubated for seven to eight months. They are laid around september and hatch in April when insects are most abundant. They raise their eggs in abandoned megapode nests (megapode are a type of bird).

Life span

Komodo dragons and vulnerable creatures and tend to hang out in trees. They take 8-9 years to mature and live up to 30 years.
