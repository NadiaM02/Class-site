Komodo Dragon FACTS

Human Interaction

Komodo dragons are at risk by illegal hunting and loss of habtitat to human settlement. They were often hunted as trophies by big-game hunters and were killed for their skins or feet to make novelties.

venom

Along with other monitor lizards komodo dragons and secrete a venomous poison and fron mouths. Their victioms suffer from low blood pressure and blood loss, sening their prey into a state of shock.

Predators

Komodo dragons are the most dominant predator and most their home islands. Young komdodo dragons prey on smaller animals in the trees such as snakes, lizards, and birds.
