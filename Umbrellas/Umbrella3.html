Name

Their came comes from rumors about there being a dragon-like lizard living in the Lesser Sundra Islands. Its Species name is Komodoensis, its Genus is Varanus and its Family is Varabidae.

What is a Komodo Dragon

A Komodo dragon is a large lizard found in the Indonesian islands of Komodo, Rinca, Flores and Gili Motang.
