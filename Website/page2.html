Balance

Balance is when a piece is weighted equally through composition and design. I feel that each of the pieces below represent three types of balance.

Symmetry

This creation was made as an example of Symmetry. I included it in my portfolio because it represented a form of balance.

Radial Symmetry

This creation was made as an example of Radial Symmetry. I included it becasue it represented a form of balance.

Asymmetry

This creation is an example of Asymmetry. I included it becasue it represented a form of balance.

Rhythm

Rhythm in art is a repettion of a pattern or element within a piece. The creations below represent different emotions or atmosphere through rhythm.

This piece represents four diferent atmosphere and emotions. The top left reprents vibration, the top right represents chaos, the bottom left represents a slow dance and the bottm right represents a soothing river. I included it becasue it taght me ways to portray emoting through simple shapes, textures and colors. 

Shape

Shape is a flat inclosed area of one colore, texture and line. The pieces below use simple shapes to portray an idea.

Sting Rays

This sting rays were made to be logos. They were made by manipulating the negative and positive shapes. 

Nessy

This piece were also made as logos. Using the negative and positive shapes to portray a message. 

Jellyfish

This piece focuses on jellyfish as positive shapes seperated from the background. 

Posters

The pieces below were made for 3 different contests. 

This poster was made for the NAIAS 2019 Detroit Auto Show. It is important becasue it is the first poster I made for a contest like this. I included it because I learned a lot from and was a project we spent a lot of time on. 

This poster was made for the annual lcc Fashion Show. It is important becasue it was the second poster I made with the purpose to advertise an event. I included it because I liked its compotions and overal look.

This poster was made and entered into an annual contest. The purpse was to promote volunteeting. I included it becasue it took a long time to make and it was the last poster I made this year.
